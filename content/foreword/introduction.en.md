# Welcome to My Creative World

Hello, visitor. I’m ++Little Corner++.

I wear quite a few hats: I have a Ph.D. in Architecture, and I’m a designer, AI product manager, AIGC artist, and a vibe coder constantly building out of curiosity. They may sound like different paths, but to me, they are all ways of doing the same thing — ==turning fuzzy ideas into things people can enter, use, and play with.==

That is why I did not turn this place into a conventional résumé. Instead, I built it as a virtual gallery — a home for my projects, articles, AI experiments, and a few strange ideas that do not yet know where they belong. You will find the spatial thinking shaped by my architectural training, a designer’s obsession with detail, a product manager’s persistent question — “Is it actually useful?” — and the spirit of vibe coding: bring it to life first, then keep making it better.

## Now on View

- **AIGC Gallery** — curious works of “art” I create with AI
- **Vibe Coding Room** — small tools that make complex creative work feel simple, such as [PromptFill](https://aipromptfill.com)
- **Notes From Corner** — ongoing observations and experiments from my WeChat publication, *AI World in the Corner*

There is no prescribed route. You can take the exhibition seriously or simply wander around — open a project, take an idea home with you, or discover an easter egg tucked away in some corner.

If you are also curious about ==AI, design, creative tools, and new things that do not have names yet==, come say hello.
